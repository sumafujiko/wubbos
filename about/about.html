The 'Wubbos' Origin Story

Wubbos is not just a collection of designs; it's a journey of creativity and heartfelt nostalgia. Born from the vivid imagination of our talented artist, Wubbos began as a childhood dream and has blossomed into a beloved brand cherished by many. Growing up in a quaint village where companionship was scarce, our artist found solace in crafting her own whimsical characters. From the tender age of seven, she poured her heart and soul into giving life to these endearing figures, each one brimming with personality and charm.


For over two decades, our artist has been honing her craft, refining her skills, and weaving enchanting tales through her artwork. With a deep-rooted passion for bringing joy to others, she embarked on a mission to share her creations with the world. Drawing inspiration from her own experiences of finding comfort in imaginary friends, she named her creations "Wubbos" as a homage to her very first character, Wubbo. These delightful characters, with their playful antics and infectious smiles, have captured the hearts of many, transcending age and time.

At the heart of Wubbos lies a desire to spread happiness and foster a sense of companionship, especially among those who may feel isolated or alone. Through a diverse range of merchandise, from cozy pillows to vibrant paintings, Wubbos offers a glimpse into a whimsical world where imagination knows no bounds. Whether adorning a child's bedroom or bringing a touch of whimsy to a cozy living space, each Wubbo and his friends carry with them a message of friendship, resilience, and the boundless power of imagination. Join us on this enchanting journey as we celebrate the magic of Wubbos and the joy they bring to all who encounter them.
